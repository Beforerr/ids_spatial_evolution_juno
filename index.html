Related works

  • Söding et al. (2001) studied the radial distribution of discontinuities in the solar wind between 0.3 and 19 AU during solar activity minimum.
  • Liu et al. (2021) studied the spatial evolution of the discontinuities from 0.13-0.9 AU, using measurements from the Parker Solar Probe.
